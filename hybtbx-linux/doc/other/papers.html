The main algorithms of the Hybrid Toolbox are described in detail in the following references:

  • Conversion of an MLD system into a PWA form

    A. Bemporad, ``    Efficient conversion of mixed logical dynamical systems into an equivalent piecewise affine form,'' IEEE Trans. Automatic Control, vol. 49, no. 5, pp. 832-838, 2004.
     
  • Discrete Hybrid Automata and the HYSDEL language:

    F.D. Torrisi and A. Bemporad, ``    HYSDEL - A tool for generating computational hybrid models,'' IEEE Trans. Contr. Systems Technology, vol. 12, no. 2, pp. 235-249, Mar. 2004.

  • Multiparametric quadratic programming algorithm:

    P. Tøndel, T.A. Johansen, and A. Bemporad, ``    An algorithm for multi-parametric quadratic programming and explicit MPC solutions,'' Automatica, vol. 39, no. 3, pp. 489-497, Mar. 2003

  • MPC for constrained linear systems and its explicit PWA computation

    A. Bemporad, M. Morari, V. Dua, and E.N. Pistikopoulos, ``    The explicit linear quadratic regulator for constrained systems,'' Automatica, vol. 38, no. 1, pp. 3-20, 2002 (Corrigendum)

  • Model predictive control of MLD Systems

    A. Bemporad and M. Morari, ``    Control of systems integrating logic, dynamics, and constraints,'' Automatica, vol. 35, no. 3, pp. 407-427, 1999.
